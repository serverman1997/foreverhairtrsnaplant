Advanced Hair Restoration Services

We offer a comprehensive range of hair restoration treatments using the latest technology for permanent, natural-looking results.

FUE Hair Transplant

Follicular Unit Extraction — the gold standard for permanent hair restoration with no linear scar and natural density.

PRP Therapy

Platelet-Rich Plasma therapy stimulates hair follicles using your own blood plasma to reduce hair fall and promote growth.

Learn More
GFC Treatment

Growth Factor Concentrate (GFC) is an advanced form of PRP that delivers higher concentration of growth factors for better results.
